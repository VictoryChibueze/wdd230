Chamber website

Purpose

The Onitsha chamber is highly dedicated to improving the commercial atmosphere in Onitsha. The chamber represents hundreds of businesses from small scale through medium scale to large scale to make Onitsha a vibrant, viable and a competitive place to do and grow business


"The Onitsha chamber website is devoted to promote economic development and tourism in Onitsha and her environs by displaying diverse range of business, attractions and events available in Onitsha.Our website serves as a guide for residents ,visitors and investors , highlighting and magnifying business opportunities and benefits of doing business in our highly economic friendly community"

Audience

"Our Chamber of Commerce hosts several networking events each month to facilitate business-to-business connections. This month, we have a Business After Hours event scheduled for [date] at [location], as well as a Networking Breakfast on [date] at [location]. You can find more information and register for these events on our Events Calendar page."


"You can easily find contact information for the Chamber's board of directors on our website. Simply navigate to the 'About Us' section and select 'Board of Directors.' There, you'll find a list of board members along with their email addresses and phone numbers. Feel free to reach out to them with any questions or concerns.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be hosting this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find information about local businesses and services offered in the area?
  • How can I become a member of the chamber and what are the benefits of membership?
